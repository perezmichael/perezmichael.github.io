THE HACKATHON

At this Hackathon sponsored by Visa and U.S. Bank, the challenge was to build innovative solutions that would help small businesses with their day-to-day operations.

Our app "Scout" helps small businesses or new business owners find the best location for their business. We used four APIs to create the application:

  • Visa Merchant Measure API - Used to see transaction growth and volume growth of similar businesses in your area.

  • Google Maps API - Used to show a map of competitors in your area, the number of competitors, and your top competitors based on reviews.

  • U.S. Bank API - Populates a small business loan rate quote.

  • Walkability API - Shows walkability score for your potential location
