OVERVIEW

Hack Big for Small Businesses was a hackathon hosted by Visa and U.S. Bank to help small business owners with their day-to-day tasks.

Over 24 hours, my friends and I built an entire web application. We called the app Scout, because it helps restaurant owners find new locations for their eatery.
